Services

Support shaped around what you're actually facing.

Every situation is different. Our services are built around the specific transitions that high-achieving professionals navigate — with precision, discretion, and genuine care.

01

Divorce & Separation

For those in the middle of it — or just beginning.

This service supports you from the moment of decision through to the other side. Whether you're certain the relationship is ending or still weighing the decision, we provide strategic clarity alongside emotional grounding.

We help you navigate legal conversations, co-parenting dynamics, financial implications, and the identity questions that no one talks about.

What's included

  • Weekly 1:1 coaching sessions (60 minutes)
  • Personalised transition roadmap
  • Between-session support via messaging
  • Access to our resource library

02

Breakup Recovery

For the ending that won't stop echoing.

Not all relationship endings come with legal paperwork — but that doesn't make them less significant. This service is for those navigating the end of a committed relationship outside of marriage, whether recent or still unresolved.

We help you process what happened, understand the patterns, and build a clear path forward — without rushing past the parts that matter.

What's included

  • Bi-weekly 1:1 coaching sessions
  • Structured recovery framework
  • Clarity sessions to process patterns
  • Forward-planning roadmap
Book a consultation

"The ending was significant. The recovery should be treated that way too."

03

Co-parenting Strategy

When the relationship ends, but the partnership doesn't.

Shared parenthood is one of the most complex ongoing relationships to navigate post-separation. This service focuses specifically on the co-parenting dynamic — communication frameworks, boundary-setting, conflict de-escalation, and building a stable environment for your children while you're rebuilding your own life.

What's included

  • Joint or individual coaching sessions
  • Communication frameworks and scripts
  • Conflict navigation strategies
  • Long-term co-parenting plan
