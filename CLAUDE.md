## Design Context

### Users
High-achieving professionals navigating divorce, separation, or breakup. Professionally capable, emotionally depleted. They want a roadmap, not a support group.

### Brand Personality
**Grounded. Precise. Human.** Warm and curious voice — asks before advising, makes people feel heard before giving direction. Coach-strategist: emotional intelligence + structured thinking.

### Aesthetic Direction
**"Measured clarity"** — editorial calm meets intelligent precision. The name "interluud" = liminal transformation: the in-between state where real change happens.

- **Fonts**: Cormorant (display serif) + DM Sans (body) — never system fonts
- **Palette**: Warm cream base · burnt sienna accent (only accent) · deep warm charcoal
- **Layout**: Asymmetric, left-aligned, generous whitespace, editorial proportions

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over comfort** — every element earns its place
2. **Strategic warmth** — warm and curious, with structure underneath
3. **Liminal by design** — asymmetry, negative space, typographic restraint
4. **The premium of restraint** — one accent, one contrast, one hero moment per section
5. **Hierarchy that leads** — headline → context → action, never three things competing

### Never
Glassmorphism · cyan/purple gradients · neon · centered layouts · system fonts · stock therapy imagery · equal visual weights
