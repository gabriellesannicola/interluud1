Who We Are

Coaching built around the complexity of what you're going through.

Our mission

interluud exists for people who are used to solving hard problems — and have just encountered one they can't think their way through alone. We bring strategic clarity and human presence to the moments that matter most.

The coach

Gabrielle San Nicola

Founder & Lead Coach

Gabrielle founded interluud after recognising a gap in support for high-achieving professionals navigating the end of significant relationships. Having witnessed firsthand how capable, driven people can become paralysed when their personal lives intersect with the relentless demands of their careers, she built a practice designed specifically to meet that need — combining rigour with genuine human presence.

With a background in [coaching/psychology — placeholder], Gabrielle brings both structure and empathy to every engagement. She works exclusively with individuals on a one-to-one basis, believing that meaningful progress in this kind of transition requires unhurried attention, not a program. Her approach is direct, warm, and firmly grounded in what her clients actually need to move forward.
